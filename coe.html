COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

CTU-Danao's College of Engineering is a hub for aspiring engineers, offering a range of exciting programs to choose from. Students can explore fields like:

Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE): Learn how to make factories and businesses run smoothly and efficiently.

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME): Design, build, and improve machines and systems, from cars to robots.

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE): Shape the world around us by designing buildings, bridges, and infrastructure.

Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BSEE): Work with electricity and electronics, powering everything from homes to computers.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE): Combine computer science and electrical engineering to design and build the hardware and software that drives our digital world.

With these diverse programs, CTU-Danao's College of Engineering is a great place to start your journey towards a rewarding career in engineering!
