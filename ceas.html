COLLEGE OF EDUCATION ARTS AND SCIENCES

CTU-Danao's College of Education is dedicated to nurturing future educators and equipping them with the skills to inspire young minds. They offer a variety of programs, including:

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd): Prepare to teach children in the early grades, fostering a love of learning and a strong foundation for future success.

Bachelor in Technology and Livelihood Education (BTLEd) major in Home Economics: Learn to teach practical skills like cooking, sewing, and home management, empowering students to become confident and resourceful individuals.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) Major in Mathematics: Become a teacher for middle and high school students, specializing in helping students master the world of numbers and problem-solving.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) Major in Science: Become a teacher for middle and high school students, specializing in engaging students in the wonders of biology, chemistry, and physics.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) Major in Social Studies: Become a teacher for middle and high school students, specializing in bringing history, geography, and civics to life, fostering an understanding of the world around us.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) major in Home Economics Technology (HET): Combine your passion for home economics with technology, teaching students about food science, textiles, and design in a modern context.

Diploma in Professional Education (DPE): Enhance your teaching skills and knowledge with this practical program, designed to prepare you for a successful career in education.

If you're passionate about education and want to make a difference in the lives of young people, CTU-Danao's College of Education is the perfect place to start your journey
