COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

CTU-Danao's College of Hospitality and Tourism Management is your gateway to a world of exciting opportunities in the travel and hospitality industry. They offer programs that will equip you with the skills and knowledge to succeed in this dynamic field:

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM): Learn the art of creating memorable experiences for guests, from managing hotels and restaurants to planning events and providing exceptional customer service.

Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management: Explore the world of travel and tourism, developing expertise in destination marketing, tour planning, and sustainable tourism practices.

If you're passionate about travel, hospitality, and creating unforgettable experiences, CTU-Danao's College of Hospitality and Tourism Management is the place to launch your career!
